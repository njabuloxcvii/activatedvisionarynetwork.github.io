ABOUT US

Who Are we ?

Activated Visionary Network is an organization that specializes in impacting and improving lives of young people by educating the youth to explore ways by which they can use career system to meet their needs. We use techniques from secondary, tertiary education and community driven developments in our programming.

Through harnessing the power of positive role models,education and community principles, activated visionary network is able to engange with young people , build their self confidence and sense of self-worth and increase their social and vocational competence

VISION

Shaping the future by providing impactful initiatives,product and services with core interest in education for our youth, discovering new knowledge and share it to the world

MISSION

Our mission is to prepare and inspire , prepare and develop the youth to appreciate the values of honesty,creativity,innovation,hard work, service and appreciation of enviroment
